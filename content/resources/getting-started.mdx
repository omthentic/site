## Understanding Your Feedback

After each session, you'll receive feedback on:

- **Content**: What you said and how well it answered the question
- **Delivery**: Your tone, pace, and confidence level
- **Structure**: How well-organized your response was
- **Empathy**: Emotional intelligence and awareness

  The AI feedback is incredibly detailed. I could see exactly where I needed to improve, and the suggestions were actionable.
</BlockQuote>

## Next Steps

Now that you're set up, here are your next steps:

1. Complete your first 3 practice sessions
2. Review your feedback and identify patterns
3. Set specific goals for improvement
4. Practice consistently (2-3 times per week)
